Step-by-Step Guide

This tutorial provides a super clear visual guide. Let's create your lively dancing person:

1. Prepare Paper Strips: Start by gathering colorful paper strips. You'll need strips for the legs, body, and arms.
2. Create the Legs: Take a longer strip (e.g., green) and fold it into a spring-like shape. This allows your dancer to "bounce".
3. Form the Body: Take another strip (e.g., red) and form it into a ring by gluing the ends together.
4. Attach the Arms: Curl or fold two strips (e.g., orange) for the arms and glue them to the sides of the body.
5. Design the Head: Draw a cute face on a yellow strip using markers.
6. Complete the Head: Curl the head strip into a ring, gluing the ends together.
7. Assemble the Dancer: Carefully glue the head onto the body, and the body onto the leg base.
